Most Shorts fail for one reason: the opening doesn’t create enough *curiosity momentum*.

## The 2-second rule
In the first 2 seconds, the viewer needs to feel one of these:
- “This is about me.”
- “I didn’t know that.”
- “I want to see the result.”

If your first line is a generic intro ("Hey guys…"), you’re asking the viewer to *wait*.
They won’t.

## A simple hook formula
Use this structure:

**Result + Time + Specific audience**

Examples:
- “Get your first 1,000 views in 24 hours (even with 0 followers).”
- “Write a 30-second script in 3 minutes (without sounding like AI).”

## The fix: swap your opening
Take your current opening and rewrite it 3 times:
1) **Outcome-first** (result)
2) **Mistake-first** (what beginners do wrong)
3) **Counterintuitive-first** (unpopular truth)

Pick the one that feels most *sharable*.
