## TL;DR
- Your hook’s only job: earn the *next second*.
- Use one of 5 beginner formulas (Result, Mistake, Contrarian, Curiosity, Checklist).
- Write 10 hooks first, then pick the best one (don’t marry your first draft).

## The problem
Most Shorts die in the first 1–2 seconds.

Beginners usually start with:
- “Hey guys…”
- “Today I’m going to show you…”
- a long intro explaining context

That’s fine for long-form. It’s poison for Shorts.

## The quick answer
Use a hook that is:
1) specific,
2) fast,
3) about the viewer,
4) hard to ignore.

Then deliver on it immediately.

## 20 YouTube Shorts hook examples (beginner-friendly)
Mix and match these. Replace the bracket parts with your topic.

### 1) Result in a timeframe
1. “I got **[result]** in **[time]** — here’s exactly what I changed.”
2. “Stop doing **[thing]** if you want **[result]** by **[deadline]**.”
3. “This is the fastest way I know to **[result]** in **[time]**.”
4. “I tried **[method]** for **[time]** — the results surprised me.”

### 2) Mistake / warning
5. “If you’re doing **[common behavior]**, you’re killing your **[goal]**.”
6. “The #1 mistake beginners make with **[topic]** is this.”
7. “Don’t buy **[tool/product]** until you hear this.”
8. “You’re not bad at **[skill]** — you’re missing this one step.”

### 3) Contrarian take
9. “Everyone says **[popular advice]**… I disagree.”
10. “You don’t need **[thing]** to **[result]** — you need this.”
11. “Stop optimizing **[metric]**. Optimize **[better metric]** instead.”
12. “This ‘best practice’ is making you slower. Here’s what I do.”

### 4) Curiosity / pattern interrupt
13. “I’m going to show you something most people never notice about **[topic]**.”
14. “This tiny change doubled my **[metric]**.”
15. “Watch what happens when I do **[weird experiment]**.”
16. “Here’s the simplest explanation of **[topic]** you’ll ever hear.”

### 5) Checklist / steps
17. “If you can do these 3 things, you can **[result]**.”
18. “Do this before you post **[type of content]**.”
19. “3 signs your **[thing]** is about to fail.”
20. “Here’s my 15-second checklist for **[goal]**.”

## Step-by-step: write a hook in 60 seconds
1. **Pick one promise** (result, avoid a mistake, save time, make money, look better, feel better).
2. **Add specificity** (numbers, timeframe, audience, constraints).
3. **Remove fluff** (no greetings, no backstory).
4. **Make it speakable** (say it out loud; if you stumble, shorten it).
5. **Write 10 variations** and pick the best.

### A simple beginner hook formula
Use this when you’re stuck:

**“If you want [RESULT] in [TIME], stop doing [COMMON MISTAKE] and do [BETTER STEP].”**

## Common mistakes (and fixes)
- **Mistake:** vague hooks (“This will change everything”) → **Fix:** add one concrete detail.
- **Mistake:** asking viewers to “wait for it” → **Fix:** show proof *immediately*.
- **Mistake:** hook doesn’t match the video → **Fix:** rewrite the hook after you edit the video.
- **Mistake:** trying to be clever → **Fix:** be clear first, clever later.

## Template / checklist
Copy/paste this into your notes before writing a Short:

- Hook promise: __________
- Audience: __________
- Specific detail (number/timeframe): __________
- Proof I can show in the first 2 seconds: __________
- 10 hook variations written? (yes/no)
