If your Gumroad product isn’t getting views, your **title** is the first lever to pull.
It affects:
- what people search
- what they click
- what they remember (and recommend)

Below are **25 title patterns** you can copy-paste.
(They’re especially effective for Notion templates, prompt packs, cheat sheets, and swipe files.)

## The simplest SEO rule
Put your main keyword **in the first 6–8 words**.

Bad:
- “The Ultimate System for Solo Creators (Notion)”

Better:
- “Notion Content Calendar for Solo Creators”

## Pattern 1: Outcome + Format
1. “{Outcome} (Notion Template)”
2. “{Outcome} (Checklist)”
3. “{Outcome} (Swipe File)”
4. “{Outcome} (Prompt Pack)”
5. “{Outcome} (Cheat Sheet)”

Examples:
- “YouTube Shorts Script Builder (Notion Template)”
- “Gumroad SEO Quick Wins (Checklist)”

## Pattern 2: Timeframe + Outcome
6. “{Outcome} in {Time} (Template)”
7. “{Time} {Outcome} Plan (Notion)”
8. “{Time} Challenge: {Outcome} (Planner)”

Examples:
- “30-Day Shorts Challenge Planner (Notion)”
- “Publish 10 Shorts in 7 Days (Checklist)”

## Pattern 3: Audience + Outcome
9. “For {Audience}: {Outcome} Template”
10. “{Audience} {Outcome} System (Notion)”
11. “{Audience} Starter Kit: {Outcome}”

Examples:
- “For Solo Creators: Content Repurposing Tracker (Notion)”
- “Coaches Lead Tracker (Notion Template)”

## Pattern 4: Use-case keyword + Format
12. “{Use-case} Notion Template”
13. “{Use-case} Prompt Pack”
14. “{Use-case} Dashboard”
15. “{Use-case} Toolkit”

Examples:
- “YouTube Shorts Ideas Notion Template”
- “Instagram Reels Hook Prompt Pack”

## Pattern 5: Niche + Asset
16. “{Niche} Hooks Swipe File”
17. “{Niche} Content Calendar (Notion)”
18. “{Niche} Script Templates (Pack)”

Examples:
- “Fitness Shorts Hooks Swipe File”
- “Real Estate Reels Script Templates (Pack)”

## Pattern 6: Problem-first (high intent)
19. “Stop {Bad Thing}: {Template}”
20. “Fix {Bad Thing} with {Format}”

Examples:
- “Stop Overthinking Hooks: 50 Hook Templates”
- “Fix Low Retention with a Shorts Script Checklist”

## Pattern 7: Bundle without saying “bundle”
(Useful when you deliver multiple files.)
21. “{Outcome} Kit (Templates + Prompts + Checklist)”
22. “{Outcome} OS (Notion + Prompts)”

Examples:
- “Shorts Growth Kit (Notion + Prompts + Swipe File)”
- “Creator Workflow OS (Notion + Checklists)”

## Pattern 8: Minimalist + specific
23. “{Number} {Things} for {Outcome}”
24. “The {One Thing} for {Outcome}”
25. “{Outcome}: {Number}-Step Checklist”

Examples:
- “50 Hooks for YouTube Shorts”
- “Gumroad SEO: 10-Step Checklist”

## Quick self-check before you ship
- Does the title contain your keyword? (Notion template / prompt pack / swipe file)
- Is it scannable on mobile?
- Would a stranger understand it in 3 seconds?
