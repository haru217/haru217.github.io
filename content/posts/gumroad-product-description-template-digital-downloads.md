Most Gumroad pages don’t convert because the description is *too vague*.
People don’t buy “a template.” They buy a **specific outcome**.

Below is a **copy/paste Gumroad product description template** for digital downloads (Notion templates, prompt packs, PDFs, swipe files). I’ll also show you exactly what to fill in—without sounding salesy.

---

## The copy/paste Gumroad description template

Paste this into your Gumroad product description and replace the parts in `{brackets}`.

### 1) Headline (1 sentence)
**Ship {result} in {time} with {product type}—built for {who}.**

Example:
> Ship better YouTube Shorts hooks in 10 minutes with a Notion swipe file—built for solo creators.

### 2) Who it’s for (3 bullets)
This is for you if:
- You are {who} and you want {goal}.
- You struggle with {pain #1}.
- You want {benefit} without {common objection}.

### 3) What you get (exact deliverables)
You’ll get:
- **{deliverable #1}** ({format}, {size or count})
- **{deliverable #2}** ({format}, {size or count})
- **{deliverable #3}** ({format}, {size or count})

> Rule: list only what’s actually inside the download.

### 4) What it helps you do (outcomes)
Use this to:
- {outcome #1}
- {outcome #2}
- {outcome #3}

### 5) How to use it (3 steps)
1. {step 1}
2. {step 2}
3. {step 3}

### 6) FAQ (keep it short)
**Q: Is this beginner-friendly?**
A: {answer}

**Q: What tools do I need?**
A: {answer}

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: {your policy, link to Gumroad policy}

### 7) Call to action (one line)
If you want {result} without {pain}, download it here.

## Fill-in guide (so it doesn’t sound generic)

### Be specific about the buyer
“Creators” is too broad.
Better:
- “solo creators making YouTube Shorts”
- “Notion users who sell digital templates”

### Use counts and file types
People trust specifics.
Instead of:
- “Includes templates and guides”

Say:
- “1 Notion template + 1 PDF cheat sheet + 20 prompt examples”

### Say what’s *not* included (optional)
This reduces refund requests.
Examples:
- “This is not a course.”
- “No video editing presets included.”

## Gumroad SEO notes (quick wins)

These aren’t magic, but they help discoverability:

- **Title:** include 1 core keyword + 1 clear outcome
  - Example: “YouTube Shorts Hook Formulas (Notion Swipe File)”
- **First 2 lines:** repeat the outcome in plain language
- **Use scannable formatting:** short paragraphs + bullets
- **Images:** show what the buyer will see after purchase (real screenshots)
