A good Gumroad product page does two jobs:

1) It helps the right buyer say **“yes”** faster.
2) It prevents the wrong buyer from purchasing and asking for a refund.

The easiest way to do both is a clear **FAQ section**.

This post gives you a **copy/paste Gumroad FAQ template** you can adapt in 10 minutes.

## Why your Gumroad FAQ matters (even for SEO)
Gumroad pages are text-heavy. That’s a good thing.

- More **relevant text** helps both Gumroad search and Google understand what you sell.
- Clear answers reduce "pre-sale" DMs, confusion, and refunds.
- A good FAQ increases conversion because it removes uncertainty.

Think of your FAQ as: *objection handling + expectation setting*.

## A simple FAQ structure that works
Use this order:

1. **What is this?** (format + what's included)
2. **Who is this for? / not for?**
3. **How do I use it?** (setup time + steps)
4. **Do I need any tools?**
5. **Can I get a refund?**
6. **License / commercial use**
7. **Support**

You don’t need 20 questions. 7–10 is plenty.

## Copy/paste Gumroad FAQ template
Paste this into your product description under an "FAQ" heading and edit the brackets.

### FAQ

**Q: What do I get exactly?**  
A: You’ll receive **[format]** including:  
- [Item 1]  
- [Item 2]  
- [Item 3]  

**Q: Is this a physical product?**  
A: No. This is a **digital download**.

**Q: Who is this for?**  
A: This is for **[target user]** who wants to **[outcome]**.

**Q: Who is this NOT for?**  
A: If you’re expecting **[something you do NOT provide]**, this is not a fit.

**Q: How do I use it / set it up?**  
A: Setup takes about **[X minutes]**.  
1) Download the files  
2) [Step]  
3) [Step]  

**Q: Do I need Notion / Canva / Adobe / any paid tools?**  
A: You need **[tools]**. A free account is **[enough / not enough]**.

**Q: Does this include future updates?**  
A: **[Yes/No]**. If yes: updates will be delivered through your Gumroad library.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Because this is a digital product, refunds are **[policy]**.  
If you run into an issue (broken file, wrong download, etc.), email **[support email]** and I’ll help.

**Q: Can I share this with my team / friends?**  
A: Please don’t. Your purchase includes a **single-user license**.  
If your team needs access, please purchase **[team licenses / multiple copies]**.

**Q: Can I use this commercially?**  
A: **[Yes/No]**. You may **[allowed use]**. You may not **[disallowed use]**.

**Q: Where do I get help?**  
A: Email **[support email]** with your Gumroad receipt and a screenshot of the issue.

## Example: FAQ tuned for a Notion template
If you sell Notion templates, these are the questions that prevent 80% of complaints:

- “Does this work on mobile?”
- “Do I need a paid Notion plan?”
- “How do I duplicate the template?”
- “How long does setup take?”

Add one extra question:

**Q: How do I duplicate the Notion template?**  
A: Click the **Duplicate** button at the top-right of the Notion page. If you can’t see it, make sure you’re logged into Notion.

## FAQ writing rules (so you don’t hurt conversions)
- Keep answers **short** (2–5 lines).
- Don’t be defensive. Be clear.
- Make expectations measurable ("setup: 5 minutes", "files: 7 items").
- If you mention something in the FAQ (like “lifetime updates”), you must actually deliver it.
